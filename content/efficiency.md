# Engineering Efficiency

Whatever happened to our velocity? When did deploys become slow, and our code seeming so brittle?


## Why software teams break down

Without proper tooling & processes, your quality suffers. When quality suffers, you'll see more bugs that distract you. You'll also accrue more technical debt which will be tricky and time consuming to solve. Then your velocity dips and your shipping speed grinds to a halt. Everyone's getting miserable and thinking of other places they'd rather be. Congrats, you're screwed!

None of these issues are anyone's fault. Not yours, and not your team. The missing piece is realizing that the way you used to do things no longer works. You now need to both pay attention and dedicate engineer time to making the rest of your team more effective.

You can achieve dramatic improvements with skilled & dedicated engineers who are allowed to bring everything they've got to bear on these effectiveness issues.

The rest of the industry are getting wise to this. Maybe you've heard of Developer Experience, Engineering Efficiency, Productivity Ops, or an Infrastructure team, but it's about improving and speeding up all of your teams and the entire process of building and shipping software at your company.
